THE INTERNET: A BOON OR A CURSE?

Internet is a global system of interconnected computer networks that uses Internet Protocol Suite for communication and sharing information between network and devices. It is a network of networks that consists of private, public, academic, business, and government networks etc. Using the internet we can send emails, photos, videos, and messages to our loved ones. It creates a communication medium to share and get information online. The Internet is the most popular and innovative creation within the world of technology. We stay connected with each other through social networking websites, email, direct messaging, and more.

The advantages and disadvantages are numerous and individualised.

BOONS OF INTERNET
  • Abundant Information: The internet provides us with an endless supply of information that continuously updates.It can provide resources on any imaginable thing.
  • Communication Platform: We can connect with our friends and family who live in any part of the world.It connects people through the web.
  • Education: Students can gain information in various domains.It helps a learner to expand their knowledge.Teachers can apply online videos in educational platforms to educate children who can’t afford tuitions.
  • E-Commerce: It helps one to shop an object from America and get it delivered to Asia, Africa or any other areas.
  • Internet Banking: We can manage our finances through just an app.This helped many individuals during COVID 19, where we had to go pay cashless.
  • Entertainment: Most folks love using their devices.Entertainment industry has expanded a lot through OTT and many other platforms
  • Social Media: Social networking is a medium to share and connect with people across the world.

CURSES OF INTERNET
  • Cyber Crimes: Hackers hack into a user’s personal information and misuse it or leak it on social media.
  • Pornography & Violence: Exposure of children to violence or pornographic images before they are mature enough can affect their mental age. Teens who are exposed to this glorify specific behaviours and pursue them in real-life.
  • Internet Addiction Disorder: People are addicted to their devices and spend more time on them without realising it.
  • Social Alienation: People spend their time online so much that they get stuck in a trap they can’t escape.They are separated from the real world and people which decreases their social abilities.
  • Health Issues: Spending too much of their time playing games or watching series can strain your eyes.It also affects their physical health as well as their mental health.

Artificial intelligence and machine learning may be technologies that manage multiple facets of our lives. The world may very well become a mix of virtual and reality, and it might be challenging at times to decipher between the two. The Internet can be an advantage and can also be a disadvantage to anyone. It totally depends on the person and how they use it. Its proper use can improve somebody’s life and its improper use can be a disaster to someone. Overall, it totally depends on the person and how he/she uses it.
